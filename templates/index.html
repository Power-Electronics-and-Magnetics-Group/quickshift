quickSHiFT (quick Simulation of High Frequency Transformers) UT Austin Power Electronics & Magnetics Group
quickSHiFT is a free, libre, and open-source to provide an easy and accurate simulation of current distribution in high-frequency transformers under the Skin effect, and to identify optimal designs for minimal power loss given certain parameters.
